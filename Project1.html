Mutton Recipe

1Kg Mutton

Ingredients

  • Yogurt for marination
  • onions
  • Tomatoes
  • ginger-garlic
  • oil/ghee
  • while spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves,)
  • powdered spices (turmeric, chili,coriander,cumin)
  • salt and water
  • garam masala and fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash mutton

    2. Clean and wash the mutton pieces well. Keep aside.

  2. Heat oil

    3. Heat oil in a cooker or pan. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf

  3. Fry onions

    4. Add chopped onions and fry till golden brown

  4. Add gingerâ€“garlic paste

    5. Add paste and fry until raw smell goes.

  5. Add tomatoes & spices

    6. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt cook till oil separates

  6. Add mutton

    7. Add mutton pieces and mix well. Fry for 5-7 minutes

  7. Add water & cook

    8. Add enough water. Pressure cook for:

    • Cooker:4-5 whistles
    • Pan:Cook covered till soft
